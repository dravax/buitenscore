What Is BuitenScore

A structured checklist-based and
case-verified governance review system

BuitenScore evaluates hospital governance across six dimensions, using documentation-based review and anonymized case sampling — producing structured comparability signals, not standardized rankings.

  • 01
    Documentation Integrity
    Verification of clinical records, structured completeness, and traceability standards.
  • 02
    Indication Appropriateness
    Evidence-based justification review of procedures relative to documented diagnoses.
  • 03
    Procedure–Diagnosis Alignment
    Structural consistency between coded diagnoses and performed procedures.
  • 04
    Clinical–Billing Consistency
    Coherence between clinical documentation and billing submissions.
  • 05
    Complication Governance
    Structured handling, documentation, and review of clinical complications.
  • 06
    Continuity Logic
    Gatekeeper simulation and referral pathway integrity assessment.

Assessment Process

How BuitenScore Works

A structured five-step process from scope definition to final Governance Alignment Score.

1
Scope Definition
Department, timeframe, and procedure volume are defined and agreed prior to assessment commencement.
2
Self Assessment
Evidence-based structured checklist completion with documentation upload via secure submission portal.
3
Case Sampling
Anonymized minimum dataset submission from a stratified sample of clinical cases within scope.
4
Three-Layer Review
L1 Structured consistency screening
L2 Clinical appropriateness review
L3 Continuity & gatekeeper simulation
5
Alignment Score
Governance Alignment Score (0–100) issued with structured breakdown and risk-adjusted interpretation.

Governance Architecture

Built for Structural Integrity

Every element of the BuitenScore process operates under documented governance controls, ensuring independence, reproducibility, and auditability.

01
Role-Separated Reviewers
Clinical, coding, and governance review functions are structurally separated to prevent conflict and ensure independent signal generation.
02
Conflict of Interest Declaration
All reviewers complete mandatory conflict-of-interest declarations prior to case assignment. Institutional relationships are disclosed and managed.
03
Methodology Versioning
All scoring methodologies are version-controlled with transparent publication dates, enabling longitudinal comparability across assessment periods.
04
QA Second-Layer Verification
Final scores undergo mandatory quality assurance review by an independent second reviewer before issuance.
05
Transparent Scoring Logic
All scoring criteria, weighting rationales, and interpretation guidelines are publicly documented in the BuitenScore Methodology Booklet.
06
Data Anonymization Controls
Case data is anonymized prior to review submission and processed under structured data governance protocols throughout the assessment lifecycle.

Legal Positioning

Scope & Limitations

BuitenScore is a governance signal instrument. All assessments are voluntary and evidence-based. BuitenScore does not:

  • ✗ Issue reimbursement approvals or payment recommendations
  • ✗ Replace or interact with national accreditation systems
  • ✗ Provide clinical advice, diagnosis, or treatment guidance
  • ✗ Act as an insurer, regulator, or legal authority
  • ✗ Generate binding compliance determinations
Core Statement
BuitenScore provides governance alignment signals only. All assessments are voluntary, conducted under structured evidence-based methodology, and issued exclusively as structured comparability data — not regulatory determinations, clinical directives, or financial instruments.
